copyright holder of the original FerrumTTY source code.

The maintainer has final responsibility for project direction, releases,
contribution acceptance, compatibility claims, and clean-room compliance.

the maintainer from continuing to offer additional licenses. Before accepting

explicit license grant. Opening an issue or submitting a proposal does not
transfer copyright.
